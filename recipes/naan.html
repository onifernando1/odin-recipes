Summary

A simple naan recipe that can be made within 45 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1x 7g sachet dried yeast
  • 2 tsp golden caster sugar
  • 300g strong white bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • Â½ tsp baking powder
  • 25g butter or ghee, melted, plus extra 2-3 tbsp for the tray and brushing
  • 150ml yoghurt

Instructions

  1. Mix lukewarm water, yoghurt, yeast and sugar. Mix and leave for 5 minutes.
  2. Mix together the dry ingedients
  3. Add yeast mixture to dry ingredients
  4. Knead for 10 minutes
  5. Roll and fry
