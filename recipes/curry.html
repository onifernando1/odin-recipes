Potato Curry Recipe

Summary

A simple potato curry recipe that can be made within 45 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups potatoes - cubed
  • 1 green chillie
  • 1 cup onions - finely chopped
  • 1 cup tomatoes - chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger - paste
  • 1 tsp garlic - paste
  • 10 curry leaves
  • 2 tsbps oil
  • 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1.5 tsp red chillie powder
  • 1/8 tsp tumeric powder
  • 1 stp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • salt to tase
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsps coriander leaves
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in pan
  2. Add mustard, cumin and curry leaves
  3. Add onions and green chillie
  4. Saute for 30 seconds
  5. Add tomatoes and salt and saute till cooked
  6. Add red chilli powder, tumeric, garam masala and corriander powder
  7. Add cubed potatoes, 1 cup of water and saute until cooked
  8. Add salt to taste
  9. Add the coconut milk
  10. Leave to simmer
